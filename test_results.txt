Models:
  Honest  — 19 real-world crops, CalibratedClassifierCV(RF400), leakage-free
  Hybrid  — 54 unified crops, confidence-adaptive blending of real + synthetic

Blending Rules:
  HIGH_CONF_REAL  — Real confidence > 85% → weights 75% real / 25% synth
  DEFAULT         — Normal case         → weights 60% real / 40% synth
  LOW_CONF        — Real confidence < 50% → weights 50% real / 50% synth

Key Observations:
  - Top-1 crop agrees across both modes in all 5 test cases.
  - Hybrid mode surfaces additional crops (gourd, papaya, date_palm) from the
    synthetic model's broader 51-crop vocabulary.
  - Soft dampening prevents synth-only crops from overtaking real predictions.
  - HIGH_CONF_REAL rule triggered in cases 2 & 5 (chickpea >85% confidence).
