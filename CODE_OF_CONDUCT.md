# 🌱 Crop Recommendation System — Code of Conduct

## 📌 Our Commitment

The Crop Recommendation System (CRS) community is committed to creating a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for everyone.

We welcome contributors from all backgrounds and aim to build a space where collaboration, learning, and innovation can thrive without fear of harassment or discrimination.

---

## 🌍 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

* GitHub repositories (issues, pull requests, discussions)
* Communication channels related to the project
* Any community interactions representing the CRS project

It applies to all participants: contributors, maintainers, and users.

---

## 🤝 Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

* Be respectful and professional in communication
* Be welcoming to people of all backgrounds and experience levels
* Accept constructive feedback gracefully
* Focus on what is best for the community and project
* Show empathy and patience towards others

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
* Offensive comments related to race, gender, religion, or identity
* Personal attacks or insulting language
* Sharing private or sensitive information without permission
* Posting inappropriate, explicit, or harmful content
* Repeated disruptive behavior after being warned

If someone asks you to stop a behavior, you are expected to comply immediately.

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Actions for violations may include:

* Warning or request for correction
* Temporary or permanent ban from the project
* Removal of contributions or comments

Severity of action depends on the nature of the violation.

## 🧠 Responsibility of Contributors

By participating in this project, you agree to:

* Follow this Code of Conduct
* Maintain a respectful environment
* Contribute in a constructive and professional manner
